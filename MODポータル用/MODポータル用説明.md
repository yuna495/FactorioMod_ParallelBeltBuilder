# Parallel Belt Builder

**Parallel Belt Builder** is a lightweight, highly intuitive tool designed to eliminate the tedium of manual main bus construction. Simply lay down a single reference belt lane, and let the mod replicate it up to 16 times in a single click!

## Why you'll love it

* **Zero Hassle, Instant Replication**:

  Place 2 to 16 parallel belts on either the Left or Right side instantly. It automatically detects and duplicates Yellow, Red, Blue, or custom modded belts.

* **Smart 90-Degree Curves**:

  Handles straight paths and direct 90-degree corners. The mod automatically extends/trims inner and outer lines for perfect connection bends.

* **Intelligent Item Fallback**:

  In Normal placement mode, if your inventory runs out of belts, the mod will automatically place the remaining tiles as **ghosts (blueprints)**, prioritizing the inner lines first.

* **Mistake-Friendly**:

  If you place a belt wrong during recording, simply pick it back up. The mod ignores deleted tiles and builds your path flawlessly.

* **Seamless Control**:

  Toggle the window instantly with `Ctrl + Alt + Q` or use the shortcut bar. Safe cancel with `Esc` key.
